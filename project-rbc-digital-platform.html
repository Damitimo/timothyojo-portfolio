RBC Insurance Digital Transformation

Led the development of a unified insurance platform that consolidates the entire advisor journey from prospect account creation to final e-application submission. Eliminated data silos and repetitive data entry across multiple platforms, resulting in increased advisor productivity and improved sales conversion rates.

The challenge

The insurance advisor journey was fragmented across multiple disconnected platforms. Advisors had to create prospect accounts in Salesforce, conduct Personal Needs Assessments (PNA) in Salesforce, perform quoting in a separate platform, and submit e-applications in yet another system. This disjointed flow required repetitive data entry, increased processing time, and created friction that led brokerage advisors to choose competitors over RBC Insurance.

Research

We conducted extensive user research with insurance advisors to map the complete journey from prospect to policy. Through interviews, workflow analysis, and time-motion studies, we identified critical pain points: repetitive data entry across four different platforms, context switching between systems, and the time-consuming nature of the fragmented process that discouraged advisors from choosing RBC Insurance.

User Research Insights & Journey Maps

Key findings

Advisors were losing 2-3 hours per application due to repetitive data entry and platform switching. 60% of brokerage advisors cited the cumbersome process as a reason for choosing competitors over RBC Insurance.

Current state analysis

The insurance journey was split across four separate platforms: Salesforce for account creation and PNA, a standalone quoting system, and a separate e-application platform. Each step required re-entering client information, creating data inconsistencies and advisor frustration. The entire process could take 4-6 hours for a single application.

Opportunity

By creating a single platform that carries information seamlessly from account creation through final application submission, we could eliminate repetitive data entry, reduce processing time, and make RBC Insurance the preferred choice for brokerage advisors. The opportunity was to create a unified experience that flows naturally from prospect to policy.

A unified platform where advisors create prospect accounts, conduct Personal Needs Assessments, generate quotes with multiple variations, and submit e-applications—all while information flows seamlessly between steps without repetitive data entry.

Solution overview

The unified platform consolidates the entire insurance journey into a single, seamless flow. Advisors create prospect accounts, conduct Personal Needs Assessments, generate quotes with multiple variations, and submit e-applications—all within one platform where information carries forward automatically, eliminating repetitive data entry and reducing processing time.

Platform Architecture & User Journey

Key features

The platform features a seamless flow from prospect account creation through e-application submission, with automatic data carryover between steps. Advisors can conduct Personal Needs Assessments, generate multiple quote variations, and submit applications without re-entering information. The system includes progress tracking, automated workflows, and real-time collaboration tools.

Impact

The unified platform reduced application processing time from 4-6 hours to 1-2 hours, eliminating repetitive data entry and platform switching. Advisor productivity increased by 40%, and RBC Insurance saw a 25% increase in applications from brokerage advisors who now preferred the streamlined process over competitors.
