RBC Digital Insurance Platform

Led the development of a comprehensive digital insurance platform that unified multiple touchpoints into a seamless omni-channel experience. Streamlined advisor workflows and improved client onboarding processes, resulting in significant efficiency gains and enhanced customer satisfaction.

RBC Digital Platform Dashboard

The challenge

RBC Insurance faced significant challenges with fragmented digital touchpoints and inefficient advisor workflows. The existing system required advisors to navigate multiple platforms, leading to increased processing time and potential errors. Clients experienced a disjointed onboarding process that didn't reflect RBC's commitment to exceptional service.

Research

We conducted extensive user research with advisors and clients to understand pain points in the current insurance process. Through interviews, surveys, and workflow analysis, we identified key friction points: redundant data entry, lack of real-time information, and complex approval processes.

User Research Insights & Journey Maps

Key findings

Advisors spent 40% of their time on administrative tasks rather than client consultation. Clients expected a modern, streamlined experience similar to other digital services they used daily.

Current state analysis

The existing system required advisors to use five different platforms for a single client interaction. Data silos prevented real-time updates, and the approval process could take up to 48 hours. Client onboarding involved multiple touchpoints with no clear progress tracking.

Opportunity

By creating a unified platform that consolidates all insurance processes, we could transform the advisor experience and significantly improve client satisfaction. The opportunity was to leverage RBC's existing technology infrastructure while creating a modern, intuitive interface.

A single platform that empowers advisors to focus on what they do best—building relationships and providing expert guidance—while delivering a seamless, modern experience that clients expect from RBC.

Solution overview

The new platform integrates all insurance processes into a single, intuitive interface. Advisors can access client information, process applications, and track approvals in real-time. Clients receive a modern onboarding experience with clear progress tracking and automated updates.

Platform Architecture & User Journey

Key features

The platform includes a unified dashboard for advisors, automated workflow management, real-time collaboration tools, and a client portal with progress tracking. Advanced analytics provide insights into process efficiency and client satisfaction metrics.

Impact

The new platform reduced client onboarding time by 40% and increased advisor productivity by 25%. Client satisfaction scores improved significantly, and the platform became the standard for all RBC Insurance digital interactions.
