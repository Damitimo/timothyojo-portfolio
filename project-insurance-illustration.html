Insurance Illustration Software

Led the modernization of RBC's legacy insurance illustration software, partnering with Novinsoft to rebuild the Windows 97-era system using modern technology and improved UX while maintaining design consistency across the RBC ecosystem.

Client

RBC Insurance

Date completed

December 2024

Role

Product Manager, RBC

Insurance Illustration Software Interface
The Challenge Competitive Landscape Partnership Strategy Design System Integration Delivery Management Impact

The challenge

RBC's insurance illustration software had a Windows 97-era interface that was severely outdated compared to competitors like IA and Manulife, who had already modernized their systems. The legacy system created a poor user experience for advisors and potentially impacted sales performance due to its dated appearance and functionality.

Competitive landscape

Our analysis revealed that major competitors like IA and Manulife had already invested in modern illustration software with contemporary UX patterns and visual design. This put RBC at a competitive disadvantage, as advisors might perceive our tools as less professional or reliable compared to the sleek interfaces offered by competitors.

Partnership strategy

We partnered with Novinsoft to rebuild the illustration software using their modern codebase and pre-wired components. While leveraging their technical foundation was beneficial for development speed, we needed to ensure the software would seamlessly integrate with RBC's existing ecosystem and maintain brand consistency.

Design system integration

One of my key responsibilities was ensuring the Novinsoft team could use their codebase while applying RBC's visual identity. I facilitated the export of our RIG (RBC Insurance Group) design system components through Storybook and Artifactory, providing the development team with comprehensive styling and theme files that maintained brand consistency across the RBC ecosystem.

RIG Design System Components & Storybook Integration

Delivery management

I managed the delivery of the project, coordinating between internal stakeholders, the Novinsoft development team, and design system requirements. This involved ensuring timely delivery of design assets, maintaining quality standards, and facilitating smooth integration between the new software and existing RBC systems.

Impact

The modernized insurance illustration software brought RBC's advisor tools in line with industry standards, improving user experience and potentially increasing sales effectiveness. By maintaining design system consistency, we ensured seamless integration with the broader RBC ecosystem while leveraging modern development practices and pre-built components for faster delivery.
